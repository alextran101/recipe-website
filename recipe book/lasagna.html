Lasagna

Lasanga is an Italian pasta dish, possibly one of the oldest. It has many layers of fillings that make it different from your traditional pasta!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzerlla cheese, sliced
  • 1/4 cup granted Parmesan cheese
    •

    Directions

    1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in dutch oven over medium heat until browned. Stir in crushed tomatos, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and peppers. Simmer, covered, for about 1 and a half hours, stir occasionally.
    2. Bring a large pot of slightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodes in boiling water for 8 - 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rise with cold water. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
    3. Preheat the over to 375 degrees F(190 degrees C).
    4. Spread 1 and a half cups of meat sauce in the bottom of baking dish. Arange noodles length wise over meat sauce. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 and a half cups meat sauce over mozzeralla, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese. Repeat and top with the remaining cheese then cover with foil.
    5. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for another 25 minutes. Rest for 15 minutes before serving.
